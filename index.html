Welcome to Wow Films UG

COMPANY PROFILE

Wow Films Ug is a filming company aimed at producing beautiful stories on screen which are wow in such a way as being educative, indulging and entertaining.

Our stories are mainly based on family and culture because we believe that as many people watch our movies across the globe, we contribute to making the world a better place through the wisdom shared in our stories.

Our company is fully registered in the government of Uganda which is our home land. We believe that everyone deserves to have a voice and that the world is a better place when we listen, share and build community through our stories. This is why we engage in discovering and developing talents from writing, acting, costuming, production designing, sound, editing, make-up and production.

OUR VISION

 To discover and develop local talents into stars as well as bringing great entertainment to the world.

OUR MISSION

To hold the banner of the film industry in Uganda and Africa at large.

OUR MOTTO

TAATA

"The gift of moral values"

Synopsis

This film is about a young man Peter having grown up with his single mother, misses out on some moral values in his upbringing which resulted into him messing up his life at campus. He runs after every girl he sees and ends up getting HIV /Aids virus which called for his turn around in life. He realized the need of a father to guide him on how to face life and the challenges there of.

On the other side we see john in the opposite, having grown up in a stable family morally gets a proper upbringing and ends up being a doctor and marrying his campus sweetheart Laura.

Ivan Mugomola

Producer / Writer / Actor

Ivan Mugomola,is a multi-talent film maker specializing in writing, Acting and production. He was born on 16th, January 1990 in Entebbe Uganda.

He developed the love and enthusiasm of acting while in primary school at Air force primary school in Entebbe Uganda where he featured in the school drama club play.

He acted in movies like Marinea. a high school tv series as teacher Jude 2024 as a supporting character Directed by Ssenyonga Meddie, produced by Pearl screen pictures Uganda.

He also acted in feature film title "TAATA" (The gift of moral values) as Peter the lead role. Where he was also the writer and producer of the film in 2024.

He also been in stage plays like "Gulma yaguuza" (The labor of love) at Bat Valley theater produced by Pearl Screen pictures Uganda.

Ivan is also an Orator and a Pastor at cornerstone community church, Entebbe Uganda.

He is a hotelier, and an entrepreneur, he has also authored a book titled BUILDING WEALTH

Henry king aka Henry Matovu

Director / Editor / Actor

Henry king aka Henry Matovu, (born 25th, June, 2000) Is a Ugandan Actor, Editor, Director, Assistant Director and Production designer, He is major force to reckon with in the entertainment scene.

Henry King was born and raised in Kampala Uganda, in a family of 5 kids being the elder son. He attained a certificate in editing in 2020 and worked on projects like Marianah, Hellena, Boyfriend and some local television shows as an editor in the past years, and Makula (certainly not a Mrs.) in which he was nominated in the category of best editor 2024 in the Uganda Film Festival 2024.

He has directed movies like wrong number series, Taata (the Gift of moral values) 2024, and has been an assistant director on projects like Junior Drama’s Club, season 1 and 2, Marianah High school tv series, Makula, to mention but a few.

He debuted as an actor in the Ugandan film theatrical play Guluma Yaguza in Pearl Screen Pictures Uganda. began his acting carrier in his early years of school till date, he is a natural talent since he hasn’t attended so many film classes in the acting prior his first appearance.

He acted in movies like YOUR TURN (2021) by Kizito Samuel Saviour SANYU SERIES(2022) by Nabwiso films,KYADALA TV SERIES,THE HOTEL,MARIANAH HIGH SCHOOL by Jude Bamundaga,Beneath Beauty,Mania,Hellena high school,When the Game goes left, TV shows like Bijju comedy on KBS TV, He is mine as a lead role ‘’Joel”,CURIOCITY as a lead role “Mambo”, First eleven as asurporting character ‘’Kimuli”, khosa(2023) as a production designer, The Care Giver(2023) as Assistant Director, Gambalagala (2023) as Assistant Director ,Makula as ERICK(2024),Why I cried at my wedding as kayembe boy (2024), Junior Drama Club season 1 and 2 as the First and second Assistant Director(2023).
